# 🏡 Real Estate Marketplace – Business Architecture (V1 Simplified)

---

## Positioning

You are building a **marketplace platform**:

- Connect customers with brokers/agents and property owners  
- Provide listings, discovery, and lead generation  
- Monetize via **lead access + visibility**  

You are **NOT**:
- A consultancy  
- A property advisor  
- A broker  

---

# 👤 POV 1 — Customer (Buyer / Tenant)

## What they can do

- Browse properties without login  
- Search by:
  - City  
  - Locality  
  - Budget  

- View property details:
  - Price  
  - Location  
  - Description  
  - Who posted (broker or owner)

- Contact via:
  - “Contact” button  

---

## Lead Submission (OTP Verified)

- Customer enters:
  - Name  
  - Phone number  

- OTP is sent to phone  
- Lead is **valid only after OTP verification**

---

## What they experience

- Simple browsing  
- Clear listing ownership (broker or owner)  
- Real, verified listings (admin-approved)  
- No duplicate listings  

---

# 🧑‍💼 POV 2 — Vendor (Broker / Agent / Owner)

## Who this is

- Brokers / agents  
- Property owners  

👉 Both are treated as **vendors**

---

## What they can do

- Create account  
- Post properties (unlimited, free)  
- Manage listings  
- Receive leads  
- View leads (based on plan)

---

## Listing Rules (Simplified)

- **One property = one listing**
- No joint listing (no broker-owner merge for now)
- Each listing belongs to:
  - Either broker OR owner  

---

## Listing Visibility

- Every listing is:
  - Reviewed by admin  
  - Approved before going live  

### Admin actions:
- Approve  
- Reject (with reason)  
- Ask for more verification  

---

## Lead Access (Core Monetization)

- Vendors can view:
  - **Only 5 leads per listing (Free plan)**  

- After limit:
  - Contact details are locked  
  - Message shown:
    > “Upgrade to unlock more leads”

---

## Subscription Model (Simplified)

### Plans:

| Plan | Leads per Listing | Price |
|------|------------------|-------|
| Free | 5 leads          | ₹0    |
| Paid | Unlimited        | ₹499–₹999 |

---

## Benefits of Paid Plan

- Unlimited leads  
- Higher ranking in search  
- Better visibility  

---

## Search Visibility Logic

### Ranking Priority:

1. Paid users  
2. Free users  

👉 Paid vendors get:
- More visibility  
- More leads  

---

## Lead Inbox

- Vendors can see:
  - Customer name  
  - Phone  
  - Property reference  

- OTP ensures:
  - Leads are real  
  - Higher conversion  

---

# 📞 Callback Request (Simplified Booking)

Instead of consultation:

- Customer can:
  > “Request Callback”

- Vendor receives:
  - Lead with callback intent  

👉 No scheduling complexity in V1  

---

# 🛠️ POV 3 — Admin (Platform Operator)

## Responsibilities

- Approve/reject listings  
- Maintain quality  
- Monitor leads  
- Manage users  
- Control subscriptions  

---

## Moderation

- Every listing must be approved  
- No listing goes live without admin approval  

---

## Plan Configuration

Admin controls:
- Lead limits  
- Pricing  
- Plan availability  

---

## Payments

- Payment provider: Razorpay  
- Users pay for:
  - Subscription upgrade  

---

## Renewal Logic

- Subscription valid for fixed duration  
- On expiry:
  - User returns to Free plan  

---

# 🔗 How System Works Together

- Customers → generate leads  
- Vendors → receive leads  
- Platform → restricts access  
- Vendors → pay to unlock  

---

# 💡 Core Business Model

- Free listings → attract vendors  
- Customers → generate demand  
- Lead restriction → creates urgency  
- Subscription → unlocks value  

---

# 🚀 One-Line Summary

Free property listings attract supply, customers generate leads, and revenue comes from charging vendors to access and convert those leads.
