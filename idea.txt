# Property Listing Platform — Business Idea

## Definitions
* **Vendor** — Property owner/agent who lists properties (supply side).
* **Customer** — User who browses and inquires about properties (demand side).
* **Lead** — A customer’s inquiry (name + phone + property reference) sent to a vendor.

---

## Core Business Flow

### Step 1: Vendor Adds Supply
* Vendor can post **unlimited properties** (FREE).
* No subscription required to list.
* This builds platform inventory.

### Step 2: Customer Generates Demand
* Customer **browses properties** (no login required).
* Customer clicks **“Contact Vendor”** and submits:
  * Name  
  * Phone number  
  * (Property reference is attached automatically.)
* This creates **one lead** for that property’s vendor.

### Step 3: Lead Delivery to Vendor
* Vendor receives for each lead:
  * Customer name  
  * Customer phone number  
  * Property reference (e.g. listing ID or title)

### Step 4: Lead Limitation (Core Rule)
* **Free vendors:** Can see full contact details for **only 5 leads per day**.
* After the 5th lead in a day:
  * New leads are still counted and listed.
  * Contact details (name, phone) are **hidden**.
  * Vendor sees: **“Upgrade to unlock more leads”** (or similar CTA).
* Counter resets daily (e.g. at midnight IST or per calendar day).

### Step 5: Monetization
* **Single subscription plan** for vendors.
* **Price:** ₹499–₹999/month (final price TBD).
* **What subscribed vendors get:**
  * **Unlimited lead access** — no daily cap; all contact details visible.
  * No “Upgrade to unlock” limit.

### Step 6: Visibility Advantage
* **Subscribed vendors:**
  * Rank **higher** in search/sort (e.g. by relevance, date).
  * Get more visibility → more leads.
* **Free vendors:**
  * Rank **lower** in same search/sort.
  * Fewer leads in practice.

---

## Summary for Implementation (MERN)
* **Vendor:** Register/login, CRUD properties, view leads (with daily limit logic), subscribe.
* **Customer:** Browse properties (no auth), submit “Contact Vendor” form → creates lead.
* **Platform:** Lead storage, daily lead count per vendor, subscription status, search/sort with subscription boost.
